DMUOptimizer

Role: Setting to handle the Optimizer parameters

The DMUOptimizer repository has the following characteristics:

  • Primary Tab Page: /Digital Mockup/DMU Optimizer/DMU Optimizer
  • Repository File: DMUOptimizer
  • Version: 1
  • Full Compatibility: 1
  • Upward Compatibility: 1

DieFaceDesignGeoSet

Role: Geometrical set creation Management

Detailed role: Management of Die Face Design Geometrical set preferences.

The DieFaceDesignGeoSet repository has the following characteristics:

  • Primary Tab Page: /Mechanical Design/Die Face Design/GeoSet

  • Full Compatibility: 1
  • Upward Compatibility: 1

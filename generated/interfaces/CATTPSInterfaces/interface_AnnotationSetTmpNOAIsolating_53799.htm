Methods


Sub IsolateLinksOfNOA()
Method responsible to remove all the links in between ditto NOA and the 2D instantiated Detail. This call focuses the remaining link in between the ditto NOA and the 2D Drafting component used for its representation. The handling is global to the authored content and results in isolating all the existing ditto NOAs.

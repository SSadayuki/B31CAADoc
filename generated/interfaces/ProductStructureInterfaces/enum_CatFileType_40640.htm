CatFileType (Enumeration)

enum CatFileType {
  catFileTypeText,
  catFileTypeMotif,
  catFileTypeHTML
}
Possible types of Files.
Values:
catFileTypeText
Bill of material is recorded in Text File.
catFileTypeMotif
Bill of material is recorded in Motif File.
catFileTypeHTML
Bill of material is recorded in HTML File.

GMModelInterfaces Framework:CATSide Macro

Macro CATSide ()
Defines the side where is the matter when walking along an element in the direction of its default orientation.
Parameters:
CATSideLeft
The matter is on the left.
CATSideFull
The matter is on both sides: the object is immersed inside a cell of higher dimension. (non manifold configuration).
CATSideRight
The matter is on the right.
CATSideUnknown
The matter side is not known.
This object is included in the file: CATTopDefine.h

VisualizationBase Framework:CATViewpointGravityMode Enumeration

enum CATViewpointGravityMode {
  NoViewpointGravity,
  SingleViewpointGravity,
  UpOrDownViewpointGravity
}
Type on gravity for a 3D Viewpoint.
Values:
NoViewpointGravity
The whole model can rotate in any direction
SingleViewpointGravity
The gravity direction stays vertical and up. The observator keeps his head vertical.
UpOrDownViewpointGravity
The gravity direction stays vertical (up or down). The observator keeps his head vertical but can have it up side down.

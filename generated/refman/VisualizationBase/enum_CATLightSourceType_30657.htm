VisualizationBase Framework:CATLightSourceType Enumeration

enum CATLightSourceType {
  LS_INFINITE,
  LS_SPOT
}
Type of light source.
Values:
LS_INFINITE
Infinitely distant like the sun,
LS_SPOT
Spotlight with a cutoff angle between 0 and 90 degrees, or equal to 180 degrees (lightbulb equivalent)

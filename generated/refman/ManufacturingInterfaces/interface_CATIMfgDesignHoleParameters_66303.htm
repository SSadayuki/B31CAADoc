ManufacturingInterfaces Framework: CATIMfgDesignHoleParameters Interface

Usage: you can freely reimplement this interface.

interface CATIMfgDesignHoleParameters

Interface to retrieve parameters from a design hole.

Role: CATIMfgDesignHoleParameters has methods to retrieve parameters from a design hole.


Method Index
GetDepth(CATICkeParm_var&) Retrieves the depth of the hole.
GetDiameter(CATICkeParm_var&) Retrieves the diameter of the hole.
GetDirection(double&,double&,double&) Retrieves the direction of the hole.
GetOrigin(double&,double&,double&) Retrieves the origin of the hole.
get_Parameter(CATString&,int&) Retrieves a parameter of the hole from its name.
get_Parameter(CATString&,double&) Retrieves a parameter of the hole from its name.

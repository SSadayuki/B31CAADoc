DraftingInterfaces Framework:CATAssProjectingMode Enumeration

enum CATAssProjectingMode {
  Ass_Contour,
  Ass_Region,
  Ass_HotPoints,
  Ass_ExtensionLine,
  Ass_DimensionLine,
  Ass_DimensionValue,
  Ass_OnDimensionLine,
  Ass_OnDimensionLineTwoPart,
  Ass_AlignedOnDimensionLine,
  Ass_AlongDirection
}
Associative projection mode.
Values:
Ass_Contour
Text boundary box is taken into account for the associativity.
Ass_Region
Do not used: Internal value.
Ass_HotPoints
Do not used: Internal value.
Ass_ExtensionLine
Extension line is the sub part taken into account for associativity.
Ass_DimensionLine
Dimension line is the sub part taken into account for associativity.
Ass_DimensionValue
Dimension value is the sub part taken into account for associativity.
Ass_OnDimensionLine
Dimension line is the sub part taken into account for associativity.
Ass_OnDimensionLineTwoPart
Do not used: Internal value.
Ass_AlignedOnDimensionLine
Dimension line is the sub part taken into account for associativity. Alignment will be preserve when dimension line will be moved.
Ass_AlongDirection
Do not used: Internal value.

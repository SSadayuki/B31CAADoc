DraftingInterfaces Framework:DftJustification Enumeration

enum DftJustification {
  DftLeft,
  DftCenter,
  DftRight,
  DftJustified
}
Justification of a text block.
Values:
DftLeft
Align all the lines of a text block to its left side.
DftCenter
Align all the lines of a text block to its center.
DftRight
Align all the lines of a text block to its right side.
DftJustified
Deprecated:
V5R18 Align all the lines of a text block to both sides (Not Implemented).

Parameters:
iPath
The path of the file.
iLanguage
The language of the file.
The available languages used in CATIA are:
  • For Windows:
    • "CGM",
    • "RASTER",
    • "EMF" (Windows Metafile),
    • "EPS" (Generic PostScript).
  • For UNIX:
    • "CGM",
    • "RASTER",
    • "EPS" (Generic PostScript),
    • "HPGL2" (Generic HP-GL/2 RTL),
    • "HP1000GL2" (HP DesignJet 1000 Series),
    • "OCE",
    • "GBR" (Gerber).
